Absolutely—you can build a game. The difficulty depends on the type of game.

Since I know your background, I wouldn't start with a large 3D game like GTA or PUBG. Instead, I'd recommend building a game that combines AI + programming, because that's much more valuable for your portfolio as an AIML student.

Is game development hard?

The hardest part isn't writing the code—it's combining several areas:

Game logic
Physics
Collision detection
Animations
UI
Audio
Saving/loading
AI behavior

Most beginner games don't require advanced math beyond vectors and simple geometry.

Would it help your resume?

Yes—if the game demonstrates technical skills.
